Privacy Policy

Effective January 3, 2021

This Privacy Policy has been created to allow you, the user / visitor, to better understand how the data we collect is used on our website.

Information we Collect

We collect certain information to assist in the moderation of our site and it's content.

The information we collect is:

  • Your IP address, to identify you,
  • A history of pages and uploads you view,
  • and... that's it!

When we Collect

We collect information when you register as a user on our website, when you return from an oAuth2 grant from Discord.

We collect feedback / suggestions to help improve our site.

Whenever you visit a page on our website, or when you view an upload, either from our website directly, or from a 3rd party (such as a hotlinked iamge on a forum).

How Information is Utilised

The information is used solely to improve our service. It is never sold or given to third party providers:

  • If you register as a user on our website, we store your Discord username, Discord ID, your Discord avatar, a Discord oAuth2 access token, and a Discord oAuth2 refresh token. This is required by our account authorization system to identify you and keep you logged in to our website.
  • When moderating our site, we may use your information to create links between data, such as uploads. This is only if a user has been caught violating our Terms of Service.

How Your Information is Protected

Nobody has access to your information other than authorised server and site administrators. None of your personal information, other than your Discord username, is displayed publicly on our site.

We do occasional audits of our site and server security, as well as keeping software up to date as often as possible, to ensure any vulnerabilities have been patched.

Cookies

We do not store any cookies on your browser.

Our site may contain cookies for 3rd party services, such as CloudFlare. These are out of our control.

Third Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties any of your personally identifiable information.

Advertisements

Although we have plans to serve advertisements in the future, we currently do not.

Removal of Your Information

You may request to have any information stored about you on our servers permanently deleted. Please contact us requesting it.

Notification of Updates

User privacy is our top priority. We will notify you at least 7 days before updates to our privacy policy on our Discord server.
